Applying for an EHIC

You are eligible for a UK-issued EHIC as you're told us that:

  • you are an EEA, Swiss or dual national
  • you were resident in the UK before 1 January 2021
  • you are not studying or do not plan to study in the EEA or Switzerland before 1 January 2021

You can also apply for eligible family members if they meet certain criteria

Next Steps

During your application we will ask you to provide the following information which we will use to confirm your UK residency:

  • your NHS Number, if you know it
  • your gender
